## Importing Lists

To import these into your app:

1. Log in as a parent
2. Go to "Lists"
3. Click "New List"
4. Add a title (e.g., "Grade 1 - Short Vowels")
5. Add each word
6. Save

## Tips for Creating Lists

- Keep lists to 10-15 words for young learners
- Group words by pattern or theme
- Start with simpler words and progress to harder ones
- Include words from their school curriculum
- Mix in some fun or silly words to keep interest

## Word Patterns by Grade

### Kindergarten

- 3-letter CVC words (consonant-vowel-consonant)
- Simple sight words

### Grade 1

- 4-letter words
- Simple compound words
- Basic sight words

### Grade 2-3

- Consonant blends
- Long and short vowels
- Common endings (-ing, -ed, -er)

### Grade 4-5

- Multi-syllable words
- Prefixes and suffixes
- Homophones

### Grade 6+

- Complex vocabulary
- Subject-specific terms
- Challenge words
